CYLINDER VISUAL INSPECTION

This program teaches facility staff about visually inspecting all cylinders. The course covers cylinders, valves, cleaning and compressor maintenance. ​

CYLINDER HANDLING

This course is designed for facility staff who handle the cylinders, and conduct functions such as filling and transfilling. Meeting all government safety requirements. ​

FIRE SUPPRESSION

The Fire suppression training focuses on cylinders most commonly used in fire suppression systems. 3AA, 4BA and 4BW. ​

CTS INSTRUCTOR

Do you have extensive knowledge and experience inspecting cylinders? Would you like to train others in cylinder visual inspections? ​

EDDY CURRENT

Are you a requalifier who needs training in the use of Eddy Current instruments? Our course covers required government regulations. ​

VIRTUAL TRAINING

Many times, due to safety regulations or inconvenient travel, a course can not be taught in person. In such circumstances we offer a virtual training program. It lacks the hands on experience but provides the necessary safety procedures and information. ​

PRIVATE TRAINING

Groups or facilities benefit greatly from having our instructor visit them at their facility. They receive personal, customized cylinder training to meet their unique needs on their schedule. The facility sets the date, time and specific needs of their locations. ​

INDEPENDENT TRAINING

Some training can be conducted in house by properly trained staff. Cylinder Training services offers an in house training program covering the handling and filling of cylinders. The program contains PowerPoint, textbook, tests and certificates. ​

SEMINAR TRAINING

CTS schedules group seminars at numerous locations throughout the year. We work with individuals to bring the training to them. Working together we bring our training to small groups of people who share the experience with others. ​
